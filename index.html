The problem

When the hospital sends them home, the hardest part begins

Acute care ends at discharge. Untrained family members are suddenly expected to manage medications, watch for complications, and keep a recovery on track — often with nothing but loose notes and memory. That weight has a name: caregiver burden, and left unsupported, it leads to burnout.

Our solution

One plan, gently guided — so no one has to hold it all in their head

KinderCare takes the recovery plan a clinic hands over and turns it into clear, timed steps. It reminds, it tracks, and it quietly catches the things that slip — so caregivers can focus on caring, not coordinating.

What's inside

Everything a recovery needs, in one calm place

Designed around real caregiver fatigue — simple enough to use at 3 a.m., thorough enough to trust.

💊

Medication tracker

Timed reminders with dose, timing, and "with food" details. Guardrails on high-alert medications help prevent accidental double-dosing.

📅

Calendar & appointments

Medications and appointments in one shared calendar, with the names of the doctors involved and what each visit is for.

🎙️

Voice assistant

Ask out loud — "When's the next dose?" or "What did the surgeon say?" — and hear the answer from information already in the app.

🩺

Care instructions

Physio tasks, wound care, and diet staging laid out as simple checklists, tailored to the specific surgery.

📈

Recovery tracker

A clear "days left" view and milestone plan, so families can see progress and know what good recovery looks like.

🆘

Emergency card

One tap surfaces medications, allergies, and surgery details to hand a paramedic or ER team — no scrambling in a crisis.

How it works

A framework that grows with the patient

🔗

Clinic integration

Routes home-tracking data directly to clinician dashboards, eliminating the hospital visibility gap after discharge.

🌿

Tailored recovery

Surgery-specific modules — progressive diet staging, swelling checklists, medication windows — with voice input to make logging effortless.

🧩

Scalable framework

A versatile data architecture that branches into pediatric, orthopedic, and general surgery recovery modules over time.
